## 🛠️ Independent Project: Retail Sales Dashboard (Power BI)

To strengthen my learning, I independently built a Power BI dashboard using a retail dataset.

---

## 📊 Project Features

- KPI Metrics:
  - Total Sales  
  - Total Orders  
  - Total Customers  
  - Total Quantity  

- Sales Analysis:
  - Monthly Sales Trend  
  - Country-wise Sales  

- Customer Insights:
  - Customer Segmentation  
  - Top Customers  

- Product Insights:
  - Top Selling Products  
  - Revenue Contribution  

---

## 🧹 Data Preparation Steps

- Removed missing Customer IDs  
- Filtered invalid data (negative quantity, zero price)  
- Created calculated column: Revenue = Quantity × Unit Price  
- Created DAX measures for analysis  

---

## 📈 Key Insights

- A small group of customers contributes a large portion of revenue  
- Certain products consistently drive high sales  
- Sales patterns show trends across different time periods  
- Geographic analysis highlights key markets
